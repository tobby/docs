Welcome to the Dojah Developer Documentation. This guide is your starting point to explore and activate Dojah’s identity verification and fraud prevention capabilities. Whether you're building a fintech app, launching an e-commerce platform, managing compliance, or scaling digital operations, our tools are designed to help you integrate seamlessly, reduce risk, and move faster—with confidence. 


<Accordion title="What is Dojah?">
  <Card title="What is Dojah?" icon="question">
    Dojah is the anti-fraud infrastructure built for the internet, helping businesses of all sizes scale securely without compromising user experience.

    We provide a seamless, secure framework that embeds fraud prevention and identity verification directly into your customer journey. With Dojah, you reduce risk while enabling frictionless onboarding and trusted user interactions.

    To achieve this, Dojah is powered by two complementary product suites:

  </Card>

   <Card title="Identity Hub" icon="user">
    A customizable identity verification infrastructure built to fit your market and use case. Whether you're verifying individuals (KYC) or businesses (KYB), the Identity Hub supports:
    - Government-issued ID verification and document analysis
    - Biometric verification (facial match, liveness detection)
    - Address verification (digital and field-based)
    - API-based and no-code widgets for fast deployment
    Each verification journey is secure, localized, and adaptable to your regulatory needs.
  </Card>

   <Card title="Anti-Fraud Hub" icon="user-secret">
    A fully integrated suite for real-time fraud detection and prevention. Built to scale with your transaction flows, this hub enables you to:
      - Monitor events with custom rules and alerts
      - Conduct AML screening and fraud scoring
      - Run credit checks and risk profiling
      - Automate fraud response from a unified dashboard
      It's everything you need to stop fraud before it starts, all in one place.
  </Card>
  
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Integration Options">
  <Card title="Integration Options" icon="link">
    Dojah meets you wherever you are in your technical stack. Choose any of the following to get started:
    - API Integration: Use clear, well-documented REST endpoints and webhooks with real-world code samples.
    - Pre-built SDKs & Libraries: Integrate verification flows instantly with SDKs for web, iOS, and Android.
    - Portal Dashboard: Create verification rules, manage workflows, perform verifications, and pull reports, without writing code.
  </Card>
</Accordion>
