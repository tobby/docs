After signing up, you will need to select a subscription plan to activate the platform's features.

**Steps to Start a Subscription:**

1.  Navigate to the Billing section, and then select the “Add New Card“ button.
    
2.  Review the available subscription tiers (Basic, Premium, Enterprise).
    
3.  Compare the plans using the detailed comparison table, which outlines features across:
    
    -   **Core Usage:** (e.g., Monthly event limits)
        
    -   **Feature Functionality:** (e.g., Access to specific dashboard tools)
        
    -   **Integration:** (e.g., API access levels)
        
    -   **Support:** (e.g., Level of customer support)
        
4.  Select the plan that best fits your business needs and follow the prompts to enter your payment details and confirm your subscription.

  <Card title="Going Live" icon="link">

  To move to **Live mode** and start verifying real users or monitoring actual transactions, your business must first be verified by our Compliance team.

    **Here's how:**

    1. Log into the Dashboard
    2. Click on “Complete Verification” on the settings tab
    3. Upload the required documentation, including:
    	- Certificate of Incorporation or Business Registration
    	- Valid means of identification for a company director
    	- Additional documents as requested based on your region or use case
    4. Our Compliance team will review and notify you via email (usually within 24–72 hours)
    Once approved, you'll gain access to:

	    - **Live API Keys**
	    - **Production environment**
	    - **Real-time data and user actions**
  </Card>
