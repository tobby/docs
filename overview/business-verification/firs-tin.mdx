This service enables businesses to validate a company’s Tax Identification Number (TIN) against 
Nigeria’s Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) records—ensuring authenticity and compliance 
before proceeding with business or financial activity.

***

#### Features :
The FIRS TIN Verification service returns authoritative business data tied to a valid tax number, including:

- Taxpayer Name – The registered legal name of the entity

- CAC Registration Number – Cross-reference to CAC registry

- Tax Office – Assigned FIRS tax collection center

- Contact Details – Phone number and email associated with the business

- TIN Format Check – Verifies structure and consistency of the submitted TIN

Yes, it provides clean, real-time verification that's excellent for onboarding vendors, suppliers, creditors, or conducting regulatory checks.

***

#### Use FIRS TIN verification when you require:

- Legal confirmation that a business is tax-registered

- A consistent match between CAC and tax authority data

- Compliance checks before onboarding vendors or partners

- Layered due diligence combined with CAC verification

- Quick validation for financial transactions or merchant signups
