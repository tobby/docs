#### Use Cases
We built EasyOnboard for businesses that want a faster, smarter, and customizable onboarding solution.
These businesses operate in industries where user verification is essential either for compliance,
fraud prevention, trust-building, or operational efficiency.

These businesses are categorized under various industries, and they include:
