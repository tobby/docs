  <Card title="Going Live" icon="link">

 ProfiledRisk is a self-service platform. You can create an account in minutes and immediately begin integrating your data sources.

-   **Sign up:** Visit the ProfiledRisk website at [**profiledrisk.com**](https://profiledrisk.com/)  
      
    
-   **Login:** Access the platform directly at [**app.profiledrisk.com/signin**](https://app.profiledrisk.com/signin)
    

Once signed in, you can configure your risk logic, and start sending events to the platform.

For business-specific enablement or higher-tier features such as advanced case workflows or enterprise SLAs, [our Sales team](mailto:sales@profiledrisk.com) is available to support your onboarding.

  </Card>


  ### Need Help?

 	If you're unsure about any documentation requirements or have questions during the verification process, reach out to our support team via support@profiledrisk.com or use the chat tool in the dashboard.
