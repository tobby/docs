The Business Lookup feature enables businesses and their customers to verify the legitimacy of other businesses, 
either through a single verification or a batch upload. Features of business lookup include:


## Features of Business lookup 

---


### Perform Single Lookup: 
The Single Business Lookup allows users to perform a real-time check on an
 individual business entity using its registration details.

### Perform Batch Lookup:
The Batch Business Lookup helps teams verify several businesses
 at once by uploading their details using a template. 

#### Steps to Perform a Batch Lookup:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click on the “Perform Batch Lookup” button to open the side modal.">
        <img src="/images/new-images/government-lookup/business-lookup/business-batch-lookup-perform.png" width="400"/>
  </Step>
   <Step title="Select the app from which the lookup is being initiated.">
         <img src="/images/new-images/government-lookup/business-lookup/business-batch-app.png" width="400"/>
  </Step>
    <Step title="Choose the Business ID Type (e.g., CAC, CAC Advanced) from the dropdown menu.">
        <img src="/images/new-images/government-lookup/business-lookup/business-batch-id.png" width="400"/>
  </Step>
    <Step title="Click on the “Click here” link to download the CSV, Excel, or Google Sheets template and populate the template with business information.">
        <img src="/images/new-images/government-lookup/business-lookup/business-batch-lookup-template.png" width="400"/>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the completed file back into the modal and click the “Lookup” button to process and generate results for all listed businesses.">
        <img src="/images/new-images/government-lookup/business-lookup/business-batch-submit.png" width="400"/>
  </Step>
