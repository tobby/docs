---
title: "Individual Lookup"
---

The Individual Lookup feature allows businesses to verify their users’ 
identities either by performing a single check or by uploading multiple records 
for batch processing. It’s designed to offer flexibility and speed for identity verification,
whether you’re verifying one user or hundreds. 

## Features of individual lookup 

---

### Perform Single Lookup: 
This is ideal for verifying one user at a time. 
This option allows businesses to run a quick, real-time check on an individual using their identity details.
