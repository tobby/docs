The final step of configuring your verification flow is choosing how you want to deliver the experience to your users. The Integration options provide flexibility based on your productâ€™s platform and user journey needs. Integration options include:

- **Web SDK Integration:**

	- Seamlessly embed the verification flow directly into your website or web application using our Web SDKs.

	- Customize the appearance and behavior to match your brand.

	- Offer users a smooth, uninterrupted experience without needing to leave your platform.

	- Ideal for businesses that want verification to feel like a natural part of their signup or onboarding flow.

- **Mobile SDK Integration:**

	- Extend the verification experience into your mobile applications (iOS and Android) with Mobile SDKs, by:

	- Ensuring a native mobile experience for users verifying through your app.

	- Leveraging device-specific features like camera access for ID and Liveness verification.

- **Flow Link Integration:**
Save flow to generate a unique, shareable link that directs users to a live verification flow.

	- No development effort needed to embed the flow.

	- Share the link via email, or SMS.
