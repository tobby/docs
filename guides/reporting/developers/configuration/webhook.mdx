Webhooks allow you to receive real-time notifications about events happening on your Dojah account.

To set up webhooks, follow these steps:


1. Go to the Dojah dashboard and click on the Webhooks tab

2. Click on the Subscribe button

3. Select the App you want to receive events for

4. Paste your Webhook URL

5. Select the Service name for the events you want to listen to

6. Click on the Subscribe button again to complete setup

Once configured, your webhook endpoint will start receiving data whenever the selected events occur on your app.
