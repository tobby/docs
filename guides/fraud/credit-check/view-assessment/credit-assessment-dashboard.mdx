The Credit Assessment Dashboard provides a comprehensive, in-depth view of a specific customer's credit history and related information. It is organized into three distinct tabs.

<img src="/images/new-images/credit-check/view-assessment/assessment-dashboard.png" width="400"/>


#### How it works

This is the detailed report for a single customer. It is organized into three tabs, allowing you to view the final credit score, upload a bank statement for spending analysis, and verify the customer's identity information.
