You can customize the behavior of your flow, from its basic settings and decision logic to the specific rules it applies to incoming events.

#### How it works

Configuration is done within the **Settings** tab of your flow dashboard. Here you can adjust metadata, define how final decisions are made based on risk scores in **Managing Decisions**, and build your logic in the **Rule Editor**.

#### Settings

This is where you manage the administrative details of your flow.

- You can edit the following:

	- Flow Name

	- Description

	- Webhook URL

	- Emails to receive case notifications



- Important Note: A flow's Use Case cannot be edited after it has been created.
